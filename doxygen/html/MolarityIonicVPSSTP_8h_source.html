24 /*!
25  * MolarityIonicVPSSTP is a derived class of GibbsExcessVPSSTP that handles
26  * variable pressure standard state methods for calculating thermodynamic
27  * properties that are further based on expressing the Excess Gibbs free energy
28  * as a function of the mole fractions (or pseudo mole fractions) of the
29  * constituents. This category is the workhorse for describing ionic systems
30  * which are not on the molality scale.
31  *
32  * This class adds additional functions onto the ThermoPhase interface that
33  * handles the calculation of the excess Gibbs free energy. The ThermoPhase
34  * class includes a member function, ThermoPhase::activityConvention() that
35  * indicates which convention the activities are based on. The default is to
36  * assume activities are based on the molar convention. That default is used
37  * here.
38  *
39  * All of the Excess Gibbs free energy formulations in this area employ
40  * symmetrical formulations.
41  *
42  * This layer will massage the mole fraction vector to implement cation and
43  * anion based mole numbers in an optional manner, such that it is expected that
44  * there exists a charge balance at all times. One of the ions must be a
45  * "special ion" in the sense that its' thermodynamic functions are set to zero,
46  * and the thermo functions of all other ions are based on a valuation relative
47  * to that special ion.
48  */
