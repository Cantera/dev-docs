33 //! A kinetics manager for heterogeneous reaction mechanisms. The
34 //! reactions are assumed to occur at a 2D interface between two 3D phases.
35 /*!
36  *
37  * There are some important additions to the behavior of the kinetics class due to the
38  * presence of multiple phases and a heterogeneous interface. If a reactant phase
39  * doesn't exists, i.e., has a mole number of zero, a heterogeneous reaction can not
40  * proceed from reactants to products. Note it could perhaps proceed from products to
41  * reactants if all of the product phases exist.
42  *
43  * In order to make the determination of whether a phase exists or not actually involves
44  * the specification of additional information to the kinetics object., which heretofore
45  * has only had access to intrinsic field information about the phases (i.e., temperature
46  * pressure, and mole fraction).
47  *
48  * The extrinsic specification of whether a phase exists or not must be specified on top of the
49  * intrinsic calculation of the reaction rate. This routine carries a set of
50  * booleans indicating whether a phase in the heterogeneous mechanism exists or not.
51  *
52  * Additionally, the routine carries a set of booleans around indicating whether a product
53  * phase is stable or not. If a phase is not thermodynamically stable, it may be the case that
54  * a particular reaction in a heterogeneous mechanism will create a product species in the
55  * unstable phase. However, other reactions in the mechanism will destruct that species.
56  * This may cause oscillations in the formation of the unstable phase from time step to time
57  * step within a ODE solver, in practice. In order to avoid this situation, a set of
58  * booleans is tracked which sets the stability of a phase. If a phase is deemed to be unstable,
59  * then species in that phase will not be allowed to be birthed by the kinetics operator.
60  * Nonexistent phases are deemed to be unstable by default, but this can be changed.
61  *
62  * @ingroup chemkinetics
63  */
64 class InterfaceKinetics : public Kinetics
